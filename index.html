How will my donation be used?

Every euro you give goes directly toward providing meals, gifts, and warm essentials for families in need this Christmas. We work with trusted local charities to ensure donations reach those who need them most.

Can I choose what my donation supports?

Yes! You can choose to fund a Christmas meal, a toy for a child, or a full family sponsorship. Just pick the option that speaks to your heart in the “Donation Options” section.

Do you accept physical gifts or only online donations?

We welcome both! You can donate toys, clothes, or food at our local collection points, or send a financial gift online. Whichever works best for you.

How can I volunteer or get involved in person?

We’d love your help! You can volunteer to wrap presents, deliver meals, or organize donation drives. Sign up via our Volunteer Form and we’ll get in touch with opportunities near you.

Can I read stories from families who received help?

Absolutely! Visit our Stories of Joy section to read how your generosity has brought warmth and hope to real families this holiday season.

Will I receive a donation receipt for tax purposes?

Yes! You’ll receive an automatic email receipt after completing your donation. You can also request a summary receipt for your yearly donations.

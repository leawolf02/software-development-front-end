**Christmas donations**

A festive web application designed to encourage donations during the holiday season. This project allows users to learn about different donation opportunities. Responsive design allows users to access the site on both desktop and mobile devices.

**How the webpage works**

On the top of the webpage, you will find the navigation bar, where you can access our contact information or read inspiring donor and recipient stories.

Just below, there is a donate button. By clicking it, you can make a direct contribution ,whether itâ€™s money, a warm meal or a toy, to support families during the holidays.

As you scroll down, you will find a video showing the impact of your donations, including how we manage contributions and the joyful reactions of families and children who benefit from them.

Further down, you can read real stories shared by donors and families who have received support through our platform. You also have options to customize your donations, such as giving a toy to a child or a meal to a family in need.

If you have any questions, our FAQ section aims to provide helpful answers. For additional support, you can always reach out via the contact information at the bottom of the page.

Finally, you can subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated and remain connected with the community you are helping through your donations, time, and love.
